About PrimeBridge Capital (PBC)

Your Trusted Private Financing Partner

Founded over 15 years ago, PrimeBridge Capital (PBC) is a private financing company built on providing fair, transparent, and flexible capital solutions. Unlike traditional banks with rigid requirements, we offer fast, flexible financing designed for real-world business situations.

As a private lender, we've helped over 5,000 businesses secure more than $1.2 billion in financing in 2025 alone. Our solutions work when traditional financing doesn't - whether you have credit challenges, need fast funding, or require flexible terms.

What sets us apart is our ability to provide solutions traditional banks cannot. We offer flexible underwriting, fast approvals (often within 24 hours), and customized terms based on your specific situation - not just a credit score.
